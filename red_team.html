Red Team

The Red Team simulates cyberattacks to test the strength of security systems. They use tools and tactics of real hackers to find vulnerabilities before malicious actors do.

1. Reconnaissance

Information gathering, footprinting, OSINT, and social engineering basics.

2. Scanning & Enumeration

Identify live hosts, open ports, and services using tools like Nmap.

3. Vulnerability Analysis

Find and evaluate vulnerabilities with Nessus, OpenVAS, and manual methods.

4. Exploitation

Use Metasploit and manual techniques to exploit system weaknesses.

5. Post Exploitation

Privilege escalation, persistence, and data exfiltration techniques.

6. Reporting

Document findings clearly for clients or stakeholders, including risk levels and recommendations.
