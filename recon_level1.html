Level 1: Introduction to Recon

Reconnaissance (or recon) is the first and one of the most crucial stages in ethical hacking. It involves gathering as much information as possible about a target before launching any actual attacks.

This phase helps attackers understand what the target looks like, including domain names, IP ranges, technologies used, emails, or even employee information. There are two main types of recon:

  • Passive Recon: Gathering information without directly interacting with the target (e.g., Google, WHOIS, social media).
  • Active Recon: Directly interacting with the target to gather info (e.g., ping, port scans).
