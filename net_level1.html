Level 1: Networking Fundamentals

Networking is at the heart of modern IT infrastructure. To understand how data flows through systems, we need to grasp the basics of networking.

In this level, we'll cover the following:

  • IP Addresses: Unique identifiers for devices on a network.
  • Subnets: Dividing networks into smaller, manageable parts.
  • Ports: Virtual gateways that allow communication between devices and services.

These concepts are fundamental for Blue Team operations like monitoring, network segmentation, and securing network communications.
